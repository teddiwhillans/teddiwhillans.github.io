Omelette


When you're coding too hard and need something quick and easy to eat

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • Milk
  • Bacon
  • Sliced Ham
  • Mushrooms
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Beat eggs and season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan with oil on medium-low heat
  3. Pour eggs into pan to cover bottom and cook until eggs have set
  4. Add in the mushrooms, sliced ham, bacon, etc. over the top of the omelette
  5. Fold omelette in half with spatula and serve
